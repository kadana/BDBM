Meet Maria Alvarez

Owner and founder of Balloon Decorations by Maria. Maria and her team have been serving the Southern California area since 2010. She is a kind-hearted,fun-loving, easy going person.

Maria is skilled in balloon decor, floral arrangements and table decorations. Through her experience she will help all her clients find the perfect decorations for all of their celebratory needs.

Our Team

Balloon Decorations by Maria is a small family run business. Our entire team is bi-lingual, speaking English and Spanish fluently. We promise to work tirelessly to create an enjoyable and magical experience for you and your guests - for any occasion. If you are preparing a professional business fair, event, birthday, wedding, or sensational Quincenera then Balloons By Maria is the best artist to choose. Our team has the experience, resources, and talent to ensure your event is not only successful - but memorable.
